About

When you want to see if an amity combination is available you either have to filter trough all the different spreadsheets or to keep checking the summaries for the different parties. Those solutions can be very overwhelming at first, and neither or those are very accessible, so here’s my solution to it.

General Summary

This summary contains all the amities where at least the first two ones are max rolls (the maximum bonus available). There are some exceptions for astoundingly good amities and for bonuses that are great at lower rolls.

Extended Summary

This summary contains all the amities with at least two good rolls, regardless of their position. Rolls can lower than max rolls to some extent.

Beginner Summaries (Crit and Accessory)

Those summaries are tailored for new players, as such they will contain, all instances where the related bonus is present in the first slot (common+), regardless of the other rolls. All the other bonuses of a given amity will be displayed as well.
